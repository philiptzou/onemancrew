Can a Lightbulb in the Mouth be Taken Out

“You can’t put a light bulb in your mouth” is an old urban legend. Copying Mythbusters won’t do, I designed, directed and shoot a short piece, showing a series of experiments and all the precautions we did. Our actor – who is a fellow editor - is known as the “bulb man” on Chinese internet ever since then.
